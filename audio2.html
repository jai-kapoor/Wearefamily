Green Chimneys

Composed by the legendary jazz pianist and composer Thelonious Monk, Green Chimneys draws inspiration from Monk's experiences at the Green Chimneys children's home in New York. The lively and spirited nature of the tune reflects the vibrant personalities and boundless energy of the children residing at Green Chimneys. Monk's distinctive piano style, characterized by angular melodies and unexpected harmonies, adds an element of unpredictability and playfulness to the composition. "Green Chimneys" stands as a testament to Monk's unparalleled creativity and his ability to evoke a sense of childlike wonder through his music.
